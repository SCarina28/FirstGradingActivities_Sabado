Mango Graham Cake Recipe

Mango Graham Cake made with delicious layers of sweetened cream, graham crackers, and Manila mangoes. Creamy and refreshing with loads of fresh fruit flavor, this Filipino icebox cake is a favorite for all occasions. This is a no bake dessert that is easy to prepare with just four ingredients. For variety you can also replace mango with different fruits or even a fruit cocktail. Graham cakes is perfect for summer and hot weather with its coolness and sweet taste.

Prep time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 12
Author: Lalaine Manalo

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream, very cold
  • 1 can condensed milk, chilled
  • 6 Manila mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 18 pcs. graham crackers (about 3 inches in length)

    • If using Mango Puree:

  • 2 Manila mangoes, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Instructions:

  1. Line 8 x 8 baking dish with parchment paper, leaving about a 1-inch overhang on all sides. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine heavy cream and condensed milk. Using a hand mixer on low speed, whip mixture until it reaches firm peaks.
  3. Gently fold in half of the diced mangoes, making sure not to deflate the whipped cream.
  4. Arrange one layer of graham crackers on bottom of prepared dish. Spoon ⅓ of cream mixture on crackers and spread to cover.
  5. Arrange another layer of graham over the cream. Spoon and spread ⅓ of the cream mixture. Repeat with another layer of graham crackers. Spoon and spread remaining ⅓ of the cream mixture on top of crackers.
  6. Arrange the remaining diced mangoes on top of the cream.
  7. Cover tightly with plastic film and refrigerate overnight. Or freeze for about 4 to 5 hours until firm.
  8. To serve, lift cake from pan using parchment paper overhang as support and transfer onto a serving platter. Cut into serving portions.
